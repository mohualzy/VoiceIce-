### 定积分

- **上下限确定的定积分是一个常数**<br><br>
- **积分的值与符号无关**，如定积分$\int_ a^{b} f(x) dx = \int_ a^{b} f(t) dt$<br><br>
- 对于广义积分，如果发散则不能用“偶倍奇0”的性质<br><br>
- 先分区间看反常，再看奇偶性化简<br><br>
- 几何法（1/4圆的面积）<br><br>
- **点火公式**![Pasted image 20251211205408.png](picture/Pasted%20image%2020251211205408.png)（n=1时，$I$ = 1）<br><br>
 
- **伽马函数**
  $$\Large\Gamma(s) = \int_0^{+\infty} x^{s-1} e^{-x} dx \quad (s > 0)$$
  有如下性质：<br><br>
 1.  $\Large\Gamma(s+1) = s \cdot \Gamma(s)$<br><br>
 2. $\Large\Gamma(\dfrac{1}{2}) = \sqrt{\pi}$<br><br>
 3. $\Large\Gamma(z) \Gamma(1-z) = \dfrac{\pi}{\sin(\pi z)}$<br><br>
 4. $\Large B(p, q) = \dfrac{\Gamma(p)\Gamma(q)}{\Gamma(p+q)}$
    其中贝塔函数$\displaystyle \Large B(p, q) = \int_0^1 x^{p-1} (1-x)^{q-1} dx$<br><br>
  5. $\Large\Gamma(z)\Gamma(z+\dfrac{1}{2}) = 2^{1-2z}\sqrt{\pi} \, \Gamma(2z)$<br><br>
  6. $\Large\Gamma(n+1) = n! \approx \sqrt{2\pi n} \left(\dfrac{n}{e}\right)^n$
